# Code of Conduct

This project follows the
[Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

In short:

- Be respectful and welcoming.
- Disagree about ideas, never about people.
- No harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Assume good intent, ask before assuming bad.

## Reporting

Email `jaberio@gmail.com` privately. Reports are read by the
maintainer only and handled confidentially.

## Enforcement

The maintainer may warn, edit, or remove contributions; restrict
commenting; or ban contributors who violate this code. Decisions are
final, but anyone affected may request a written explanation.
